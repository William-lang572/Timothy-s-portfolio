ISAAC TIMOTHY

ABOUT

Isaac Timothy is a singer-songwriter redefining what it means to be a modern Sri Lankan artist. With his powerhouse vocals, commanding stage presence, and fearless fusion of rock, pop, and Sri Lankan sounds, Isaac has proven he’s more than just a competition success story, he’s a rising star shaping his own lane in the industry.

After capturing hearts on The Voice Sri Lanka Season 3 2025 under the guidance of Coach Raini Charuka, Isaac’s journey has been unstoppable. He reached the finals of the show, winning audiences over with his passionate performances and emotional storytelling. His ability to balance raw power with genuine vulnerability has made him stand out as an artist who can both move and electrify a crowd.

Now, Isaac is stepping into a new chapter of his career. His debut Sinhala single, "Akaalaye", a collaboration with Mentertainment, produced by Dilru Costa (Cozzy) and written by Yashodha Adhikari. The song blends his signature rock-pop edge with the richness of Sri Lankan music, marking a bold new direction for him. Following that, he’s set to make more English and Sinhala drops, before unveiling his first album next year.

Off-stage, Isaac is grounded and introspective, a thoughtful creator who values connection as much as performance. On-stage, he’s a force of nature, known for his intensity, charisma, and the ability to make every song feel like a personal story.

DEBUT SINGLE – "AKAALAYE"

Isaac released his debut single “Akaalaye” with M Entertainment on October 18, 2025, across all major digital platforms. He worked with producer Dilru Costa (cozzy.mp3), lyricist Yashoda for the track, and director Sahan Wickramaarachchi for the music video.

The single received overwhelmingly positive feedback, with fans praising Isaac’s vocals and fresh sound. The fans are excited for the next releases and his debut album, which promises to mark a new era in the Sri Lankan music industry.
